How Commissions Work

Level 1 - Direct Referrals (12%)

Earn 12% commission when someone you directly referred makes an investment. This is your first-level referral network.

Level 2 - Indirect Referrals (2%)

Earn 2% commission when your referrals' referrals make investments. This is your second-level referral network.

Level 3 - Extended Network (1%)

Earn 1% commission from your third-level referral network. Build a strong network to maximize your passive income.

Commission Payment

Commissions are credited to your account balance instantly when your referrals make investments. You can withdraw your commission earnings anytime.
